## Core functionality

1. View cook on your large screen
2. Understand what goes on inside your meat, by looking at all your sensor's data
3. Easily pinpoint when the probe is not inserted in the meat
4. Explore your cook's temperature at your preferred unit's by changing between Celsius/Fahrenheit
5. Plan your next cook, by adding notes to your previous cooks
6. Highlight a note to view temperatures at a given note
7. Share your temperature data with friends, by exporting a PNG image

## Requirements

This app supports MacOS Catalina or later, on Intel and M1+ series machines

## Running

1. Clone the project
2. Open and run in Xcode 15.x
